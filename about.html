Play, Explore, & Learn, LLC is a respected local early childhood intervention organization. Our system of service is designed to support families of children ages birth to kindergarten who have ( or are at risk for) special needs.  We engage the families within our communities to provide services needed to facilitate happy children.  This is why we put families first.

 

Our Mission is to provide early intervention for families and children identified by the local Children's Developmental Service Agency (CDSA) ages 0 -3 years through the implementation of developmental strategies.  Monitored activities will address cognitive, social/ emotional, adaptive, and/or physical skills to include educating and supporting parent(s) or guardian(s) to lessen the effects of the developmental delay or disability of the child within the natural environment.

 

Play, Explore, and Learn, LLC is a limited liability company that is governed by the policies and procedures of the
